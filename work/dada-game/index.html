Dada Game

May 08, 2015 • zerozero

Dada game is a 21st century take on the classic Surrealist/Dadaist parlour game ‘Exquisite Corpse’ in which players take turns to draw elements of a body – head, torso, legs. The drawing is passed around using social networks and when complete will be uploaded to a public gallery.
